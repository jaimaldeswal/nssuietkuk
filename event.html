EVENTS

Republic Day Celebration

On the auspicious occassion on 71th Republic day, being children of our motherland India we celebrated our independence with great patriotism.

Self Defense Workshop 2020

“Self-defense is not just a set of techniques; it’s a state of mind, and it begins with the belief that you are worth defending.”-Rorion Gracie NSS UIET KUK organized a “Self defense” workshop at UIET, KUK on 17th February, 2019. The workshop was primarily set up for girls from classes. The workshop included various exercises and drills. The instructor also described various instances of threat and how they could tackle it.

Plantation Drive

Plants are yet another form of life which suffer itself to rejuvenates life in other living creatures. With the responsibility of protecting them as well as other forms a plantation drive was conducted by NSS UIET KUK to plant more and more trees.

Blood Donation 2019

Let's speak in louder voice, Blood-donation make our choice. Let's follow our heart and give life to someone's heart and smile to many.!

Yoga Session

Yoga helps us in developing physical, mental and intellectual personality. It also makes us strong and peaceful so NSS UIET KUK organized a yoga session in girls and boys hostel for one week.

Clean India

Adhering to the principle of father Gandhi's idea of Swatcha Bharat has motivated us. Our practices are to promote proper sanitaion and maintain a sense of swatchta throughout the campus.

Village Meet 2019

Our NSS family headed towards the village to light up hopes among the people of village. The people were briefly introduced about the function of NSS and how it would help them in moving forward towards a bright tomorrow.

Election Awareness 2019

Volunteers of NSS UIET KUK have conducted an awareness drive in nearby area of our Campus to Promote the Participation of Public in General Elections 2019, With Display of Various Poster and by Personal Approach. This Drive aims to strengthen our Democracy and which will directly impact the development of Nation.
